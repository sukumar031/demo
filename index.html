About the Trust

Rooted in Faith,
Guided by Tradition

Founded in 2024 under the guidance of Brahmashri Sriram Ghanapathigal, the Trust nurtures Vedic learning, cultural values, and spiritual awareness among future generations. Our mission is to safeguard the timeless wisdom of the Vedas through the traditional Guru–Shishya Parampara and to provide a platform for spiritual, educational, and charitable activities.

ब्र
Brahmashri Sriram Ghanapathigal
Founder · Salem

Completing his Aksharabhyasam to Ghanantham studies at Veda Bhavan, Hyderabad, Brahmashri Sriram Ghanapathigal established this Trust with a vision to carry forward the sacred flame of Vedic knowledge for generations to come.

Our School

SVBS Yajur Veda Patashala

The Trust operates a traditional Yajur Veda Patashala with students from various parts of India. Following the ancient Gurukula system, the Patashala emphasises discipline, devotion, Vedic chanting, Sanskrit learning, and character development — nurturing the next generation of Vedic scholars in an environment of sacred learning.
